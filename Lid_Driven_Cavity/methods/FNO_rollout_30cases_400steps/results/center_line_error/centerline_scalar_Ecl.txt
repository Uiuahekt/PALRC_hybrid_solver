Absolute Centerline Ensemble-RMS L2 Losses

Definitions:
  Hu(y,t) = sqrt(mean_i((u_pred_i(0.5,y,t) - u_ref_i(0.5,y,t))^2))
  Hv(x,t) = sqrt(mean_i((v_pred_i(x,0.5,t) - v_ref_i(x,0.5,t))^2))

  E_u_cl = mean_t sqrt(mean_y Hu(y,t)^2)
  E_v_cl = mean_t sqrt(mean_x Hv(x,t)^2)
  E_cl   = 0.5 * (E_u_cl + E_v_cl)

Note:
  These are absolute centerline ensemble-RMS L2 losses.
  They are not relative L2 losses.
